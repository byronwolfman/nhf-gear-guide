# Starter Kits

Ok wow, look at all the stuff in the sidebar. That's a lot of decisions to make. What if you could instead buy most of it all in one go?

Starter kits are an easy way to get (almost) fully geared. [Superior Fencing kits](https://www.supfen.com/products/full-hema-kits.html) are reasonably priced and include almost everything you need. Note that these kits do not include masks (though many _do_ include mask overlays) and not all kits include gloves or mits.

Starter kits are usually made-to-order, with production and shipping taking anywhere between 3-6 months in my experience.

## Fit

This will be a bit of a recurring theme. While Superior Fencing stuff tends to be of decent quality the fit isn't always perfect. These kits offer custom sizing on the jacket and pants for no additional charge which is extremely important if you're not shaped like a rectangle. When taking your measurements, err on the side of a relaxed fit, since a tight fit could prevent you from getting both arms above your head, something which is critical for longsword.

If you have doubts, err on the side of a more basic jacket, and think of it as your _first_ jacket, but not your last.

## Protection

The protection itself is as good as some of the more "premium" makers, and you will often see Supfen gear at tournaments. While you might sacrifice some "fit and finish" with Supfen gear, you're not giving up protection.
